Stop and Click the Flowers

Gabby Garcia


I created a short series of glitched photos to go alongside my quick flower P5.js sketch. For both of these installments, I took a personal approach, creating something I would find beauty in. For this reason, I titled the project "Stop and Click the Flowers," as it’s a twist on the original saying "Stop and smell the roses." By doing this, I’m stressing the importance of taking time out of one’s busy schedule to relax, enjoy, and appreciate the beauty that lies in front of them..

This is also shown in the glitched photos, some of which were taken during times when I personally was burnt out or not feeling my best mentally. Even then, I still created glitches in the photos that are abstract and interesting to look at, emphasizing that there is beauty even in imperfections. Some of the photos feature glitches on images of nature, as I struggle with reconnecting with the outdoors and often find myself indoors.
